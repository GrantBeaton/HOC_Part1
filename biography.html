Jack Kilby

Jack Kilby is a renowned electrical engineer known for his many important contributions to computer science and technology as a whole

Jack Kilby was born November 8th, 1923 in a small town called Great Bend, found in Kansas of the United States. He passed away June 20th, 2005, at the age of 81 years in Dallas, Texas, of, you guessed it, the United States.
